Arrow Key Controls
* Arrow keys to move.
* Z to fire.
* X to slow down time.
* C to use a bomb to clear the screen of bullets.
* P to pause.

WASD Controls
* WASD to move.
* B to fire.
* N to slow down time.
* M to use a bomb to clear the screen of bullets.
* P to pause.

Universal
* F to toggle FPS display.
